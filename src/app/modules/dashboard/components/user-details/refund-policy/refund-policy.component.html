Refund Policy
Our services are based on a business model where products are served on a network platform and the customer is charged based on their subscription or consumption. Given the nature of our services, we do not offer a refund or credit on subscription.

We will assess your refund request based on factors keeping in mind the digital nature of our services and products. There is no obligation to provide a return or credit under the following situations:

  • You have changed your mind about a particular service or product
  • Service was subscribed by mistake
  • Lack of expertise to make the best use of service or product
In order to file a refund request, you can contact us at support@hypercap.ai within 7 days from the date of subscribing to get your refund processed. Based on the usage of services the refund amount will be processed and disbursed within 7 working days. The mode and currency of the refund will be the same as the original mode of payment.
